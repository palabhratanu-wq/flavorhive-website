Who We Are

Powering India's Next Great Restaurants

FlavorHive was born from a simple belief: great food should never be held back by infrastructure challenges or capital constraints. We are India's premier restaurant infrastructure partner — handling everything from kitchen equipment to interior design, POS systems to ventilation — so restaurateurs can focus entirely on their craft.

Operating on a transparent OpEx (Operational Expenditure) model, we eliminate the massive upfront investment traditionally required to open a restaurant. We design, install, and maintain — you run the show.

What We Offer

A Complete Suite of Restaurant Services

Everything you need to launch, run, and scale — under one roof.

Full Infrastructure Setup

From kitchen equipment and exhaust systems to furniture, flooring, and lighting — we design and install the complete physical restaurant space tailored to your cuisine and capacity.

  • Commercial kitchen equipment
  • Interiors & ambiance design
  • Ventilation & fire safety

OpEx Rental Model

Zero upfront capital expenditure. We invest in the infrastructure; you pay a predictable monthly operational fee. This preserves your working capital for hiring, inventory, and growth.

  • Flexible rental tenures
  • No hidden costs
  • Scale up or down easily

Smart Pricing Strategy

Our pricing analysts study your market, competition, and food costs to build a menu pricing strategy that maximises margins while remaining competitive and attractive to diners.

  • Menu cost engineering
  • Competitive benchmarking
  • Demand-based pricing

Marketing & Branding

From social media campaigns and Zomato / Swiggy optimisation to local advertising and loyalty programs — our marketing team builds your brand and drives consistent footfall.

  • Social media management
  • Aggregator listing boost
  • Brand identity & collateral

Technology & POS

We integrate modern POS systems, order management, KDS (kitchen display systems), and analytics dashboards so you get real-time visibility into every aspect of the operation.

  • POS & billing systems
  • Kitchen display screens
  • Sales & inventory analytics

Ongoing Maintenance

Our dedicated maintenance team ensures all equipment is serviced, up-to-date, and running at peak performance — with guaranteed SLAs and rapid on-site response times.

  • Preventive maintenance
